Our Story

“The Golden Wheat brings the warmth of Ukrainian hospitality to a refined dining setting. Our journey started with a simple grain of wheat, symbolizing life and the rich soil of our homeland. We combine ancient family recipes with modern culinary techniques to create an unforgettable experience. Come and enjoy the soul of Ukraine in an elegant, sun-drenched atmosphere.”

Signature Dishes

Golden Wheat Borscht

Description: A rich, slow-cooked beet soup with tender beef prime rib, served with smoked sour cream and garlic-infused pampushky.
(Борщ з яловичим ребром, підкопченою сметаною та часниковими пампушками)

Wild Mushroom Varenyky

Description: Handmade dumplings filled with forest mushrooms and caramelized onions, drizzled with truffle-infused oil.
(Вареники з лiсовими грибами та трюфельною олiєю)

Kyiv-Style Duck Confit

Description: Our sophisticated take on the classic Chicken Kyiv, using tender duck leg, herb-infused butter, and a crispy golden crust.
(Котлета по-київськи з качиної ніжки з вершковим маслом та травами)

Slow-Roasted Pork Belly

Description: Succulent pork belly glazed with honey and mustard.
(Запечена підчеревина в медово-гірчичній глазурі)

Baked River Trout

Description: Fresh trout stuffed with garden herbs and lemon, roasted in a salt crust to preserve maximum tenderness.
(Річкова форель, запечена з травами у соляному панцирі)
