Vertical Climb 2026

Climb Up Arena

October 12-14

The Ultimate Climbing Event

Vertical Climb 2026 is the most anticipated climbing gathering of the year, bringing together professional athletes and passionate beginners. Whether you are interested in lead climbing, bouldering, or speed climbing, our event offers workshops led by world-class coaches. This year, we focus on safety techniques, mental preparation, and physical endurance. Join us at the Climb Up Arena for three days of gravity-defying action!
